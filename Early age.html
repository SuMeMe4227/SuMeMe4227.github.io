    Centineo was born in Miami, Florida, to Kellee Janel and Gregory Vincent Centineo, a businessman who executive produced the animated film Legends of Oz: Dorothy's Return (2013). He is of Italian and German descent. Centineo was raised in Boynton Beach, Florida, with his older sister, Taylor. He attended BAK Middle School of the Arts for his elementary education, and then Boca Raton Community High School for ninth and tenth grade, where he played soccer. In 2012, he left Boca Raton in order to move to Los Angeles.
